About US

A16 Wedding Organizer is one of the well-known events organizing company in the Indonesia. We have successfully organized and supervised more than 250 events, ranging from birthday parties to weddings to corporate functions, baby showers, press releases, and even some of the most highly recognized award nights. Our network of suppliers, vendors, photographers, singers, dancers, makeup artists, entertainers, caterers, and designers is extensive and growing with each event we take over. Each of them has a role to play in making the event a grand success, and we appreciate their dedication and talent. A16 Wedding Organizer has always been more than just an event organizing company. We are creative artists whose aim is to help our clients in making their dreams turn into reality. Our commitment is unmatched in the industry. All you need to do is handover the job to us and enjoy the celebrations. A16 Wedding Organizer will take care of everything.
